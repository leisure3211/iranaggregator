Executive Summary

OSINT for Iran‚ÄìU.S. escalation relies on four core evidence streams: movement & positioning, imagery & geolocation, signal/metadata trails, and documentary/financial trails. Analysts combine these with pattern models to produce warnings, likelihood scores, and attributions.

Core OSINT Approaches

  • Geospatial & Imagery (IMINT): Using Planet, Maxar, etc., for change detection at military sites (Natanz, Fordow) to spot construction or vehicle counts.
  • Narrative & Media (NARINT): Monitoring IRGC, state, and proxy media for shifts from de-escalatory to escalatory language.
  • Technical Tracking: Using ADS-B for aircraft build-ups, AIS for naval repositioning, and prediction markets for crowd-sourced probability.

Historical Case Studies

2019-2021 Tanker Incidents: Analysts used AIS and satellite imagery to show ships operating "dark" or doing ship-to-ship transfers to evade sanctions.

Jan 2020 Soleimani Strike: OSINT used social media geolocation and flight data to verify the strike site, timeline, and monitor reactions.
